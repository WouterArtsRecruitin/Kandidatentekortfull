## üì± PLATFORM SPECS QUICK REFERENCE

### Facebook Feed
- Image: 1080x1080 of 1200x628
- Video: 1:1 of 4:5, max 240 min
- Text: 125 chars primary, 27 chars headline

### Instagram Feed
- Image: 1080x1080
- Video: 1:1 of 4:5, max 60 min
- Text: 125 chars, no links in caption

### Instagram Stories
- Image/Video: 1080x1920 (9:16)
- Video: max 15s per story
- Swipe up link available

### Instagram Reels
- Video: 1080x1920 (9:16)
- Duration: 15-90 seconds
- Trending audio helps reach
